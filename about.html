About RoboCupJunior

RoboCupJunior is an international educational robotics initiative for young students, encouraging learning through robotics, programming, teamwork, and innovation.

Learning through robotics

RoboCupJunior helps students build real technical skills while also developing creativity, communication, and problem-solving abilities.

About Us

What is RoboCup?

The RoboCup organization is run by a dedicated team of volunteers, researchers, professionals, and more worldwide. RoboCup provides opportunities for students from age 10 to 19 in Junior leagues and students from age 19+ in a Major division.

What is RoboCupJunior?

RoboCupJunior is a project-oriented educational initiative that sponsors local, regional, and international robotic events for young students. It is designed to introduce robotics learning through an interdisciplinary approach for primary and secondary students.

RoboCupJunior stands apart because of its international collaboration and strong community of practice in robotics skills worldwide since 2000.
