Team Requirements

A team must be a minimum of 2 with a maximum of 4 members and 1 teacher/mentor.

OnStage competition league allows up to 5 members per team.

Team Rules

A team member(s) and/or robot(s) cannot be shared between teams.

A team can compete in only one league.

Registration Fee

Registration Fee is $350 per team.

Each student will receive a T-Shirt and a branded silicone bracelet.
