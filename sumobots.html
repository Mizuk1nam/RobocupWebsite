SumoBotsRules

Four robots are set on the corners of an octagon trying to push one another out in a battle royal. Last robot standing wins.

Rules

Match Rules

  • Four robots compete on an octagonal ring, attempting to push one another out.
  • A robot is eliminated when it does not move for more than 10 seconds or all wheels exit the ring.
  • The referee applies a colour label to each robot for identification.

Ring & Robot Limits

  • The ring is approximately 150 cm from side to opposite side.
  • The ring base is white with a 2 cm black octagonal border along the perimeter.
  • A reversal of colours is possible.
  • The ring is elevated off the floor, making it difficult for a robot to return once it moves beyond the line.
  • Robots are subject to a 22 cm diameter and height limit.
  • The robot mass must not exceed 1000 grams or 1 kg.

Points

  • A robot receives 5 points for each opponent it pushes out of the ring.
  • A robot receives 20 points for being the last robot in the ring.
  • Teams are combined together and must pick one robot to compete in a new surprise challenge presented at RoboCup that builds on the existing challenges.
