What is RoboCup Junior?

RoboCup Junior is a project-oriented educational initiative for students up to age 19. Through exciting competitions in soccer, rescue, and performance, students develop skills in programming, engineering, and teamwork.

About RoboCup Junior Canada

Inspiring the next generation of innovators through robotics education

Welcome to RoboCupJunior (RCJ), a unique, project-driven educational initiative designed to ignite a passion for robotics in students up to age 19. RCJ hosts local, regional, and international robotics events, guiding students from their first steps in robotics to meaningful, sophisticated projects.

Whether you’re just starting out or looking to deepen your skills, RCJ provides the ideal platform to grow in an inspiring, challenge-driven environment.

Teamwork

Students collaborate in teams, learning to communicate effectively and leverage each other's strengths.

Innovation

Challenges encourage creative problem-solving and innovative thinking in robotics design and programming.

Skill Development

Participants develop technical skills in programming, engineering, and electronics through hands-on experience.

Competition

Friendly competition motivates students to excel while learning sportsmanship and resilience.
