Rules

Track & Robot

  • The robot follows a black curved line on a Formula 1 race track.
  • The black line will be approximately 2 cm wide.
  • Robots are subject to a size limit of 22 cm in diameter and height.

Timing

  • This is a timed challenge.
  • Teams are allowed 3 attempts to complete the track.
  • The referee retains the best score.
  • The timer starts once the robot starts moving.

Points

  • The score is based on the time to get from the beginning to the end of the track.
  • Fast, accurate, and reliable line following is the main goal.
