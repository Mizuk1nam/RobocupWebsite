MazeRules

Robot needs to drive through a walled corridor without touching the sides as fast as possible.

Back to Leagues →

Rules

Robot & Field

  • There are no restrictions on robot size.
  • All walls, including the entrance and the exit, are at least 30 cm apart.
  • Walls are at least 15 cm high and painted white.
  • Walls may be removed, added, or changed just before a run starts to prevent pre-mapping.
  • Each round will feature a new maze design, while organizers avoid changing the level of difficulty.

Attempts

  • Teams are allowed 3 attempts to complete the maze.
  • The referee retains the best score.
  • Maximum time per attempt is 120 seconds.
  • If a robot gets stuck, the team captain may return it to the entrance area.
  • Returning the robot cancels wall bump deductions, but the countdown continues.

Points

Final score per round = Time - Bump

  • Bump = -5 points every time the robot touches a wall.
  • Completion time = time to get from beginning to end of maze.
  • Time = 120 - completion time.
  • Example: If a robot exits the maze in 40 seconds having bumped the wall twice, it earns 120 - 40 - 5 - 5 = 70 points.
